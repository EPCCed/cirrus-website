We always welcome user feedback on all aspects of the Cirrus service, both positive and negative. We use feedback provided to improve the services we deliver. 


Feedback can be emailed to the service desk using the [support email address](mailto:support@cirrus.ac.uk).


If you do not feel that your feedback has been satisfactorily addressed, please let us know by sending an email to the Director of HPC Services Director, [Josephine Beech-Brandt](mailto:j.beech-brandt@epcc.ed.ac.uk)
